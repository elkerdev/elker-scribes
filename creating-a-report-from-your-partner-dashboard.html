Creating a Report from your Partner Dashboard

1. Navigate to https://kpmg.elker.com/partner/kpmg

2. To find an organisation you're looking for, click the "Search" field.

3. Type some or all of the client's name.

4. Once you've found your organisation, click 'New report'

5. Select the correct form from the dropdown list.

Tip: If your login has lapsed from inactivity, you might be prompted to login again. Click 'Login via SSO' when prompted and you will be logged in and redirected to the correct starting page.

6. Click "Start"

Tip: Fill out as many details in the form as you can. The questions vary from form to form and can be edited at any time prior to submitting the report.

7. Select yourself or another contact from the list.

8. Click "Select" when the field expands to confirm your choice.

9. Click "Continue"

10. Review your information, or edit it as you need. Click 'Submit' when you're ready to save the report.

Tip: This completes the report submission process.
